INTRODUCTION

SAVISTAR GLOBAL EXPORT is an indigenous private limited liability company incorporated under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. The Company has its head and operations office at Plot 2181, Block 38A, David Adekunle Crescent, Amuwo-Odofin Industrial Layout GRA, Lagos, Nigeria with affiliate subsidiaries in the United States, United Kingdom, and United Arab Emirate.

SAVISTAR GLOBAL EXPORT is licensed to carry out businesses in Agro-Allied Food commodity Export, Refined Petroleum distribution and supply, Solid & Precious Minerals, and Marine Transportation. Over the years, the company has grown to become a formidable player in the global Agro, Energy, and Minerals market, servicing a wide range of customers spread across several continents. With a dedicated and talented Management Team, Trade, Operations, Finance, Trade Finance, Business Development/Marketing, and Risk Management Units, the company can identify supply and demand opportunities through an in-depth understanding of the regional and global energy markets. We take pride in the effective management and synchronization of different Units to properly analyze and mitigate the risk of doing business in identified markets, thereby producing maximum operational efficiency and profitability.
