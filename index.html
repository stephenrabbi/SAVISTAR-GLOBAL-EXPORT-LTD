Welcome to SAVISTAR GLOBAL EXPORT LTD

SAVISTAR GLOBAL EXPORT LTD is licensed to carry out businesses on Agro-Allied Food commodity Export, Refined Petroleum distribution and supply, Solid & precious Minerals and Marine Transportation.

The company has over the years, grown to become a formidable player in the global market on Agro, Energy and Minerals, servicing a wide range of customers spread across several continents. With a dedicated and talented Management Team, Trade, Operations, Finance, Trade Finance, Business Development/Marketing and Risk Management Units, the company is able to identify supply and demand opportunities through an in-depth understanding of the regional and global energy markets.

Our Core business Divisions

AGRO SUPPLY & EXPORTATION

We are registered with the Nigerian Export Promotion Council (NEPC) and as a result of our experience in Agricultural Produce buying & selling, we are in a better position to source the commodity during its season, buy and move a substantial volume to our store or that of the Inspection Company as the case may be for quantity and quality checks, handling and efficient storage before shipment. With affiliate warehouses across Nigeria particularly in areas where large quantities of quality agricultural products are produced such as grains (Sorghum and Maize) in Gombe, Gum Arabic in Maiduguri, Cocoa beans in Igobazua, Cashew nuts in Ajaka, Soyabeans in Kano while Lagos serves as the receiving hub for these commodities

SOLID MINERALS & PRECIOUS STONES

There is no doubt that Nigeria is blessed with so many mineral deposits. As at the last count, the federal ministry of mines and steel development have found over five hundred {500} location of mineral deposit in the country and identify Nine {9} that they would want to concentrate on and promote. These include Iron ore, Coal, Tin Ore, Bitumen, Gold, Columbite-Tantalite, Lead/Zinc, Wolframite and industrial minerals. At SAVISTAR GLOBAL EXPORT we cover the whole process of Exploration, Mining Operation, Processing and Commercialization of Minerals & Metals

SOURCING, PRODUCTION, PROCESSING & SUPPLY

Over the years SAVISTAR GLOBAL EXPORT LTD reputation for quality commodities has attracted the attention and patronage of several corporate blue-chip customers who buy raw commodities as input to their manufactured products. Such customers include: NIGERIA BREWERIES, GUINESS, SONA BREWERIES PLC, OLAM, PREMIER FEED MILLS

PETROLEUM SUPPLY DERIVATIVES & MARINE LOGISTICS SERVICES

We are strategically positioned in the Petroleum & Energy Sectors in West Africa with partners worldwide in all major energy hubs with strategic supply alliances to ensure continuous and reliable delivery of oil products without interruption at competitive prices. We handle Industrial supplies to major Oil Rig companies and have transport/logistic supply relationship with major telecommunication firms in maintaining constant fueling of their cell sites. We have built an efficient and effective logistics handling capacity for special transportation of goods and services. Our transportation service cuts across marine transportation. We understand the dynamics and importance of effective transportation to the survival of business. Our transportation service includes the provision of Oil vessels and barges for the delivery of petroleum products, Oil tank ships, Oil boats, Oil tugboats, Oil rig boats and supply boats. Our clients enjoy smooth delivery of petroleum products through any of the channels to designated storage facilities within and outside Nigeria.
