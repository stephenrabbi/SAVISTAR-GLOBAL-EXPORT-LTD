Petroleum & Marine Logistics

We are strategically positioned in the Petroleum & Energy Sectors in West Africa with partners worldwide in all major energy hubs with strategic supply alliances to ensure continuous and reliable delivery of oil products without interruption at competitive prices.
We put specific focus on clients that are end users of petroleum products in the aviation and industrial sectors. In addition we are proactive in anticipating existing and new client’s needs based on historical trends, air traffic and the correct reading of economic cycles in the markets. We handle Industrial supplies to major Oil Rig companies and have transport/logistic supply relationship with major telecommunication firms in maintaining constant fueling of their cell sites. We have built an efficient and effective logistics handling capacity for special transportation of goods and services.
Our transportation service cuts across marine transportation. We understand the dynamics and importance of effective transportation to the survival of business. Our transportation service includes the provision of Oil vessels and barges for the delivery of petroleum products, Oil tank ships, Oil boats, Oil tugboats, Oil rig boats and supply boats. Our clients enjoy smooth delivery of petroleum products through any of the channels to designated storage facilities within and outside Nigeria.
